Accuracy method

How comparisons stay evidence-first

Every comparison follows a strict protocol so you get facts, not guesses.

🎯
Same variant

Compare the same brand, product, flavour, pack type, and current market version.

🏷️
Label proof

Publish ingredient and nutrition differences only after label photos or trusted evidence URLs are added.

⚖️
Normalized values

Nutrition is checked per 100 g or 100 ml so serving sizes do not distort the comparison.

🩺
No medical claims

Health notes are limited to neutral consumer-awareness language after full verification.
